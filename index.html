Welcome

Welcome to NBA Stat Race! Our stats visualizer will plot for you the route a given stat took over the course of a given season for the selected players, leading up to the "winner" in that stat by season's end. As a fan, you too can now follow along a player as they try to beat out their colleagues in a stat race.

How to Use

  1. Select the season you want to follow
  2. Select the stat you want to track
  3. Search for the player(s) you want participating in the race (first and/or last names)
  4. Click on the player's name and he will then be added to the graph
  5. Hover over the graph to see the cumulative totals per player up until the desired game


  1. To remove a player, click on the next to their names
  2. If you change any of the fields, the graph and fields will glow red to let you know the next graph will be reset with the corresponding stats
